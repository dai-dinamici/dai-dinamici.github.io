The selection committee of the 
**[Michael Brin Dynamical Systems Prize for Young Mathematicians](https://science.psu.edu/math/research/dynsys/dynamical-systems-prize-young-mathematicians)** 
is seeking nominations of outstanding candidates for the 2026 prize (the 6th edition) which will be 
awarded this Fall. 

The prize was established by Anatole Katok in the fall of 2015 to recognize outstanding contributions 
to dynamical systems made by researchers within a few years of their Ph.D. The eligibility for the prize 
is limited to researchers within 4 years of their Ph.D. at the beginning of the prize calendar year.
Work not yet accepted for publication in refereed journals can be considered for the award. However, 
complete proofs must be available as preprints. 

**Web page of the prize:** <https://science.psu.edu/math/research/dynsys/dynamical-systems-prize-young-mathematicians>

Please send your nomination, accompanied by a short motivation, to [Giovanni Forni](mailto:gforni@umd.edu) 
by **May 31st, 2026**.
