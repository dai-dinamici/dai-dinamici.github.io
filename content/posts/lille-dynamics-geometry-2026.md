The **Lille Dynamics and Geometry Conference**, which will take place on **September 8-10, 2026** at the [Laboratoire Paul Painlevé of the University of Lille](https://math.univ-lille.fr/) (France).

The confirmed speakers are: *Karen Butt* (University of Chicago), *Albert Fathi* (ENS Lyon), *Ursula Hamenstädt* (University of Bonn), *Or Landesberg* (Hebrew University of Jerusalem), *François Ledrappier* (Sorbonne University), *François Maucourant* (University of Rennes), *Gabriel Rivière* (University of Nantes), *Federico Rodriguez-Hertz* (Penn State), *Ralf Spatzier* (University of Michigan), *Daren Wei* (National University of Singapore).

The day of Wednesday will be dedicated to celebrating the retirement of Livio Flaminio.

Further details and registration information are available on the Laboratoire Painlevé website:
<https://math.univ-lille.fr/detail-event/conference-lilloise-de-geometrie-dynamique>
or directly on the conference website:
<https://www.mathconf.org/gd2026>

The deadline for registration is **July 1, 2026**.
