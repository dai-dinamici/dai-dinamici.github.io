**[The Long Run--Topics in Ergodic Theory](https://math.pku.edu.cn/teachers/lizq/seminars/ergodic_theory/)** 
is a new international online seminar organized by *Ilia Binder, Jacopo De Simoi, Zhiqiang Li, 
Federico Rodriguez-Hertz,* and *Mariusz Urbański*. The seminar focuses on ergodic theory and runs on the 
second Tuesday of each month, featuring two speakers each time. 

The organizers invite anyone interested to participate and to volunteer talks.

To get notified of upcoming seminars, send an email to <subscribethelongrun@hotmail.com> with the subject 
*“Subscribe”*.
