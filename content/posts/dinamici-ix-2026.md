We are happy to announce the ninth edition of our workshop [**DinAmicI IX**](https://indico.sns.it/event/146/)!

This is the biennial workshop of [DinAmicI](https://www.dinamici.org/), the Italian (and not) group of dynamical systems. 

After the previous workshop overseas, at IMPA, Rio de Janeiro, Brasil, we are back in Italy. DinAmicI IX will take place at the *Centro De Giorgi, Pisa* on **October 19-22, 2026.**

All information is available at the website: <https://indico.sns.it/event/146/>

Deadline for registration: **August 25, 2026**.

We hope to see you at our biennial main event!
