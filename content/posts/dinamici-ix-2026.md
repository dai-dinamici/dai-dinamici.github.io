We are happy to announce the ninth edition of our workshop [**DinAmicI IX**](https://indico.sns.it/event/146/)!

This is the biennial workshop of [DinAmicI](https://www.dinamici.org/), the Italian (and not) network of researchers in dynamical systems. 

After the [previous workshop overseas, at IMPA, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil](https://impa.br/arquivo_base/eventos-do-impa/2024-2/dinamici-in-rio-dynamics-applications-interactions/), we are back in Italy. DinAmicI IX will take place at the *Centro De Giorgi, Pisa* over the period **October 19-22, 2026.**

All information is available at the website: <https://indico.sns.it/event/146/>

Deadline for registration: **August 25, 2026**.

We hope to see you at our biennial main event!
