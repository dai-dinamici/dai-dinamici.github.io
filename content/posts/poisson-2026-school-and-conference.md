This is to announce the **Poisson 2026** Summer School and Workshop, which will be held in August in Belgium:

- Poisson 2026 Summer School: 3-7 August 2026, Antwerp, further information on <https://www.uantwerpen.be/nl/personeel/sonja-hohloch/private-webpage/conference-workshop/poissonschool2026/>
- Poisson 2026 Conference: 10-14 August 2026, Leuven, further information on <https://wis.kuleuven.be/events/2026_poisson/poisson2026>

This is the 14th in a [series of international meetings](https://www.lpthe.jussieu.fr/~dito/poissongeometry/index.php?page=home) on Poisson geometry aiming at bringing together researchers interested in Poisson geometry in a broad sense from all over the world.

There are separate registration forms for the school and for the conference. The deadline for junior support to apply for support is **March 8, 2026**.

We refer to the website <https://wis.kuleuven.be/events/2026_poisson/poisson2026> for the list of speakers and additional information. Two additional workshops are in the planning for the month of June. More details will be posted on the above webpage, as they become available.
