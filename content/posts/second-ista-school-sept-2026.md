This is to announce the **"Second ISTA Summer School in Dynamical Systems"**, which will take place on **September 28-30, 2026**, in Strobl (Austria).

The school will consist of three mini-courses given by Kostiantyn Drach, Anna Florio , and Alfonso Sorrentino. 
There will also be a small number of contributed talks and a poster session.

Please find all the information on the following website: <https://dynamics-summerschool.pages.ist.ac.at/>

For junior participants selected for the school, we shall cover local expenses and contribute to travel expenses up to approximately 300 Euro. 

Applications to attend the school are open until **June 1, 2026** through the above website. Due to limited spots, we encourage those interested in participating to register early.
