The **Second ISTA Summer School in Dynamical Systems** will take place on **September 28-30, 2026**, in Strobl (Austria).

The school will consist of three mini-courses given by *Kostiantyn Drach, Anna Florio, and Alfonso Sorrentino*. 
There will also be a small number of contributed talks and a poster session.

The organization will cover local expenses of junior participants selected for the school, and will contribute to their travel 
expenses up to approximately 300 Euro. Due to limited spots, interested parties are encouraged to register early.

**All info and application form:** <https://dynamics-summerschool.pages.ist.ac.at/>

**Deadline for application:** June 1, 2026.
