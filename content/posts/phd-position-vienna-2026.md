The *Mathematics Institute* of the *University of Vienna* advertises a a PhD position starting November 1st 2026 (or first
available date after that) supervised by Professor Henk Bruin on a project involving billiard dynamics.

This position is for 3 years, with extension of up to one year subject to financial conditions in 2029-2030, and should lead to
obtaining the degree of PhD.


**Salary level:** Salary is paid in accordance with the collective bargaining agreement
(B1, 30h/week). In Vienna a PhD position is standard for 30 hours per week, and the rest
is meant to follow courses for at least 25 ECTS for the duration of the
position (i.e., up to the thesis defense).

**All info:** <https://www.mat.univie.ac.at/~bruin/FWF_Project_Billiards.html>

Applications should include:

- Application letter + CV

- Master Diploma + transcript (or equivalent)

- Two letters of recommendation (you can ask your references to send
their letter directly as pdf attachment)

**Deadline for application:** August 21st, 2026
