The Faculty is looking for candidates with an excellent track record and with the potential to establish an internationally leading research group in one of the topical modern areas of analysis, including, for example, partial differential equations, calculus of variations, geometric analysis, or harmonic analysis. GADeS Award 2023

All those who have obtained the title of PhD from an Italian University in the five calendar years preceding the year of the Award, i.e. not before 1 January 2018, can participate.
