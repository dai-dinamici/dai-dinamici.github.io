Postdoc position at Università di Padova

December 29, 2023  [expired]
A 18-month postdoc position (assegno di ricerca) is available at the Department of Mathematics “Tullio Levi-Civita” of the University of Padova, within the PRIN project “Stability in Hamiltonian Dynamics and Beyond”. The postdoc will work on the topics of the project, particularly on: Hamiltonian and non Hamiltonian (e.g., nonholonomic, conformally symplectic, almost-symplectic) systems, including integrability and (extensions of) KAM and Nekhoroshev theories.
