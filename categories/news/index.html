DAI Day V - save the date

The next edition of the biennial DInAmicI Day, DAI Day V, will take place on December 18, 2023 in Bologna. The location will be Aula dei Poeti, Strada Maggiore 45. There will be seminars by Giovanni Canestrari, Irene De Blasi, Davide Ravotti, Matteo Tabaro. The general assembly of the DinAmicI community and of the UMI Group DinAmicI will also take place. The event is organized by Anna Miriam Benini, Mirko Degli Esposti, Simone Paleari.
