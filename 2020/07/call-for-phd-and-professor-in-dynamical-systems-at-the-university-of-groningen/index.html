Call for PhD and professor in Dynamical Systems at the University of Groningen

The Bernoulli Institute of the University of Groningen (NL) is advertising a 4-year PhD position in dynamical systems, to work on the combination of multi-timescale dynamical systems and networks. The application deadline is August 31, for more information please refer to https://www.rug.nl/about-ug/work-with-us/job-opportunities/?details=00347-02S0007TEP

There is also a call for an Assistant Professor (tenure track) or (preferably) Associate Professor, with a strong track record in Dynamical Systems. The deadline for the application is August 16. For more information please refer to https://www.rug.nl/about-ug/work-with-us/job-opportunities/?details=00347-02S0007RTP

Both positions will be embedded in the Dynamical Systems, Geometry and Mathematical Physics group.
